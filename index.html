We Are Explorers

If you consider yourself to be a newcomer or a seasoned pro in the world of web development, there is always something new to explore. Whether it'd be the newest build system, tool or framework, we constantly find ourselves having learn new things and take our skills to the next level.

My name is Michael Kornblum and I'm a front-end developer from Bentonville, AR. I've studied web design and front-end development for the better part of a decade. But with that being said, there is always new territories for me to explore.

Michael Meets the Web chronicles my journey, as I move past the theorhetical knowledge of web development into the more practical aspects of the industry. They will take the form of YouTube videos, blog posts and additions to my on-site portfolio.
